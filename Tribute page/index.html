Tribute to Dora Akunyili

Who was Dora Akunyili?

Dora Akunyili was a black woman who reform Nigeria's public health sector.Motivated by the loss of her sister to fake insulin medication,Prof.Akunyili sought to reform the health sector

Early Life

Akunyili was born in Makurdi,Benue State.Akunyili graduated from the University of Nigeria in 1978 with a degree in pharmacology and PhD in ethnopharmacology.

Contributions

As the DG of NAFDAC,she was an integral part of the campaign that worked tirelessly and with significant sucess for the eradication of counterfeit drugs and unsafe food in Nigeria.

Quotes

"The founding fathers of Nigeria had a dream of building a united,prosperous,and developed nation-state where social justics reigns."
"Women tend to be less corrupt and more focused."

Conclusion

Dora Akunyili was the kind of leader Nigeria needed.
